How It Works


User 1

has a washing machine planned to do the laundary of his whites today, but realised that he has only 4kg of clothes and his washing machine capacity is 8kg.

User 2

doesn't have a washing machine yet and purchased laundrocash from us. She lives very near to User 1. She has 4 kg of whites to be washed today.

Washer

Maps User1 with User2. User 2 gets her laundary done. User 1 gets 4 Laundrocash which he can spend in future to get his laundary done.
